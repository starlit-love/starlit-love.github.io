New Beginning

July 6, 2020July 6, 2020

This will be my little corner of the internet where I can post whatever I want. I don’t have to rely on another site, I don’t have to deal with ungodly Twitter compression and whatnot. Twitter doesn’t care about artists, the cropping thing is abysmal. In the end, the site isn’t made for what I’m doing really, it doesn’t support music file embeds either. Here, I can weave art, posts, and music all in one – which is much better for what I wanna do. Plus, I get to have a little place I can call my own, which is pretty cool.
